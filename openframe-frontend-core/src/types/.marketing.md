Defines comprehensive TypeScript interfaces for Flamingo's Marketing AI system, covering campaign management, AI-generated content, social media automation, and usage tracking across various platforms and providers.

## Key Components

**Core Campaign Types**
- `MarketingCampaign` - Main campaign entity with content source configuration
- `CreateCampaignInput`/`UpdateCampaignInput` - Campaign CRUD operations
- Support for various content sources (product releases, blog posts, case studies)

**AI Content Management**
- `AIContent` - AI-generated content with approval workflow
- Content types: blog posts, social posts, ad copy, email
- Publishing controls with URL injection preferences and override capabilities

**AI Media Generation**
- `AIMedia` - Generated media assets (images, videos, Figma exports)
- Multiple providers: DALL-E, Midjourney, Flux, Stable Diffusion, video models
- Comprehensive metadata tracking including costs and dimensions

**Social Integration**
- `SocialAccount` - Multi-platform social media account management
- `ScheduledPost` - Automated post scheduling with status tracking
- Support for Slack, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube

**Workflow Management**
- `ContentApproval` - Content review and approval system
- `AIUsage` - Cost and token tracking across AI services
- Campaign statistics and performance metrics
